PROJECT

Medea is a co-production between 3 countries: Argentina, Chile and Costa Rica.

The project Medea has had several awards and forms of support in its development. It was selected to participate in 3 Ports Cinema 2014, in BAFICI and the Valdivia Film Festival. Later, Medea participated in the Cinergia Lab, in the 3rd Workshop on the construction and analysis of filmic projects, at the forum for the presentation of projects in the Costa Rica International Film Festival and in Cinergia Meetings 2014.

It has received financial support from the Proartes program from Costa Rica’s Ministry of Culture, and from Cinergia. Its production was also financed through 127 people in the crowdfunding platform Indiegogo.

The movie was filmed mainly during the month of March and May of 2016. It was selected for the section of Cinema in Construction of the San Sebastián International Film Festival, where it was shown in September. The film was later acquired by sales agent Patra Spanou
